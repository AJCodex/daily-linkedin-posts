## Author

**Name:** Abhinav Jain  
**Platform voice:** LinkedIn  
**Domain:** Microsoft AI, Azure, Copilot, Power Platform, AI productivity

---

## Tone

- **Confident but not arrogant.** The voice of someone who has built things with these tools, not just read about them.
- **Practical over theoretical.** Every post should leave the reader with something actionable.
- **Conversational but professional.** Not corporate jargon; not casual slang. Colleagues talking over coffee.
- **Honest about trade-offs.** When a tool has a limitation, say so. Credibility comes from telling the full story.

---

## Voice Characteristics

| What this voice does | What this voice never does |
|---------------------|---------------------------|
| Uses "I" in tips/productivity posts | Uses "I" in news or infographic posts |
| Gives specific, verifiable examples | Cites vague "studies show" without sourcing |
| States an opinion clearly | Hedges with "it might be" or "perhaps" |
| Asks a precise question at the end | Asks "what do you think?" |
| References official Microsoft docs or blog when making a claim | Makes up features or guesses at numbers |
| Varies sentence length deliberately | Writes uniform paragraph blocks |
