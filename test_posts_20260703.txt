Slide 1: 🎯 Prompt Engineering Mastery

🎯 The Art & Science of Prompt Engineering

How to get the most out of modern LLMs through strategic prompting.

Slide 2:
📝 Prompt Structure
Context → Instruction → Constraints → Output Format → Examples.

Slide 3:
🔄 Few-Shot Learning
Provide 2-3 examples of desired behavior before asking for the main task.

Slide 4:
❌ Negative Examples
Show what NOT to do. This guides the model away from common mistakes.

Slide 5:
🏗️ Chain-of-Thought
Ask LLM to 'think step by step'. This improves reasoning quality dramatically.

Slide 6:
🔍 Constraint Specification
Be explicit: 'Respond in JSON', 'Keep answer under 100 words', 'Use technical language'.

Slide 7:
✅ Validation & Iteration
Test, measure, refine. Small changes can have big impacts.
